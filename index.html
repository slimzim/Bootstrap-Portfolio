About Me

Hey, I'm James. Welcome to my website - I designed it myself! I'm currently enrolled in a full stack coding bootcamp through Vanderbilt University with the hope of becoming a professional web developer by the end of 2020. With the world in its current state of turmoil it's hard to predict the future, but this is a dream I'm trying my hardest to keep alive! Quarantine is a good time to practice coding.

I live in Nashville with my family. For the last 13 years I've worked in the music business, but not the part you might think - I've been mostly a classical musician. I played Principal Clarinet of the Nashville Symphony for twelve years, and I was also an active educator, clinician, and studio musician. It was a fun stretch of life being a full-time musician, but I think it's the perfect time to change gears. I'm enjoying the challenge of learning to code - it's like learning a new instrument!

Thanks for visiting my site! Feel free to check out my portfolio or send me a message. Have a great day!
